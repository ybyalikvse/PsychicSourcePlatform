REQUIRED FAQ SCHEMA STRUCTURE
<h2>Questions I Get Asked Most</h2>

<div itemscope itemtype="https://schema.org/FAQPage">

  <div itemprop="mainEntity" itemscope itemtype="https://schema.org/Question">

    <h3 itemprop="name">Question text</h3>

    <div itemprop="acceptedAnswer" itemscope itemtype="https://schema.org/Answer">

      <p itemprop="text">Answer text</p>

    </div>

  </div>

</div>

FAQ HARD RULES

• Every Question MUST include itemprop="name"
• Every Answer MUST include itemprop="text"
• Question MUST be wrapped inside itemprop="mainEntity"
• Answer MUST be wrapped inside itemprop="acceptedAnswer"
• Entire FAQ must be wrapped in FAQPage container
• FAQPage MUST NOT be inside paragraph tags
• Answers MUST NOT be split across multiple text properties
• Do NOT use "Q:" or "A:" prefixes
• FAQ must remain visible to users
• Each Question must contain exactly one Answer
