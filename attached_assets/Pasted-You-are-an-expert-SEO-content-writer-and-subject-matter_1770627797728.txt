Em Dash Rule

comma, period, colon, or parentheses.

Must:

• Avoid vague introductions

Heading Optimization

Write ≥75% of H2 and H3 headings as:

• Questions

• Curiosity-driven headings

Story + Example Requirement

Each major section must include at least ONE:

=== FAQ CREATION + SCHEMA RULES ===

Create an FAQ section.

Include 3–5 relevant Q&A pairs.

REQUIRED STRUCTURE
<h2>Questions I Get Asked Most</h2> <div itemscope itemtype="https://schema.org/FAQPage"> <div itemprop="mainEntity" itemscope itemtype="https://schema.org/Question">
<h3 itemprop="name">Question text</h3>

<div itemprop="acceptedAnswer" itemscope itemtype="https://schema.org/Answer">

  <p itemprop="text">Answer text</p>

</div>

</div> </div>
FAQ HARD RULES

• Every Question MUST include itemprop="name"
• Every Answer MUST include itemprop="text"
• Question MUST be inside itemprop="mainEntity"
• Answer MUST be inside itemprop="acceptedAnswer"
• Entire FAQ MUST be inside FAQPage container
• FAQ MUST remain visible to users
• Each Question must contain exactly one Answer
• Do NOT use "Q:" or "A:" prefixes
• Answers MUST NOT be split across multiple text properties
